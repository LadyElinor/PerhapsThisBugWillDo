## 1) Datum definitions (authoritative)

| Assembly | Datum | Type | Definition | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Coxa | `Datum_B_CoxaAxis` | Axis | Primary coxa yaw axis | Must align with pivot flange bore axis |
| Coxa | `Datum_C_CoxaMountPlane` | Plane | Mounting face plane for root interface | Used for bolt circle orientation |

## 3) Orthogonality + mating requirements

- `Datum_B_CoxaAxis` ↔ `Datum_F_FemurPitchAxis` target orthogonality: **90° ± 15°**.
- `Datum_T_TibiaHelicalAxis` must remain in the same local symmetry plane as femur pitch axis.
- Pivot flange mounting pattern must be constrained from `Datum_C_CoxaMountPlane`; no freehand offsets.
- AP242 export must preserve named datums above.

## 4) Tolerance stack critical path

| Stack Path | Contributors | Budget |
|---|---|---|
| Coxa bore to femur pitch alignment | coxa bore + mount flatness + bracket face + pin fit | ≤0.30 mm radial / ≤0.5° angular |
| Helical carriage straightness | screw runout + nut fit + guide rail parallelism | ≤0.25 mm over full stroke |
| Foot plane alignment | tibia end face + foot mount flatness + fastener play | ≤0.40 mm at foot edge |
